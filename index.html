Course Reflection

What worked well over the last eight weeks and aided your learning process?

The structured organization of the course and the practical examples provided by the instructor were very helpful.

What would you change about the course?

I would include more interactive activities, such as projects to deepen the understanding of the material.

Did you learn what you expected to learn in this course?

Yes, I learned a lot about structuring websites, using CSS effectively. These skills were exactly what I hoped to gain.

What concepts were new to you, and what concepts have you already encountered in the past?

New concepts included JavaScript integration. I had prior experience with basic HTML and CSS but learned more advanced styling techniques in this course.
